"I just want to keep working hard, stay humble, and keep trying to win games."

Damian Lillard is a professional basketball player that plays for the Portland Trailblazers. He is from Oakland, California and went to Oakland High School. Despite going to a smaller college at Weber State, he went on to get drafted as a lottery pick in the 2012 NBA Draft. He won Rookie of the year that same season! He is a six time allstar, and a six time All-NBA selection! He has played for the Portland Trailblazers for his entire career. For more info
