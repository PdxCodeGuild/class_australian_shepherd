Welcome to the biography of Damian Lillard

Damian Lillard is a professional basketball player that plays for the Portland Trailblazers. He is from Oakland, California and went to Oakland High School. Despite going to a smaller college at Weber State, he went on to get drafted as a lottery pick in the 2012 NBA Draft. He won Rookie of the year that same season!
