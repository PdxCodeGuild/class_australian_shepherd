Born in the 8th Millennium B.C., the God Emperor is an immortal telepath, who has watched and guided mankind throughout it's history. Throughout his years, he had decided that all of humanity that had spread across the vast reaches of Space during the Dark Age of Technology. It was at this point he began creating his army, through genetic splicing, he created his sons, the Primarchs, as well as forging the gene-seed which all Space Marines would be enchanced with. This began his great war across numerous planets and galaxies wiping the Xenos out of existance, so that man may live peacefully.


However strong willed the Emperor of Man is, this did not stop the forces that control the Warp to infect his sons with Chaos. Thus began the Horus Heresy, which would result in his defeat against the Traitor legions assualt on Terra (formally Earth). However, even as his life hangs on by but a thread, he continues to guide and protect the Imperium of Man from his Golden Throne, not truly being alive or dead now. This does not stop his powerful telepathic mind from continuing to guide and protect humanity from all that would try to destroy it now.
